Information

Arkeno Organisation is a vibrant, online-first, community-driven organisation. At Arkeno, we explore diverse fields including chess, coding, sports, and more. We foster collaboration, learning, and creativity, and aim to provide a welcoming online space for everyone. We also offer AWPD Webpages and other cool features! Members can stay engaged by earning Arkeno Tokens which is a small reward-based token currency.
